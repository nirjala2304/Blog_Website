Vanilla Cakes

Well, the name comes from the method used in making sponge and buttercream. Originally, the French vanilla flavour name was given to an ice cream where in yolks were used to make custard-based ice cream. Similarly, since we use yolks in the sponge to provide that custardy flavour, the cake is called French Vanilla.
